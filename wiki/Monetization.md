# Monetization

## Ads

**Rules:** NO ads during a match. NO interstitials for Battle Pass owners. Rewarded = opt-in only.

| Ad Type | Placement | Cap | Opt-In | Battle Pass |
|---------|-----------|-----|--------|-------------|
| Rewarded | Post-results: '+3 gems?' | Player decides | YES | Also available |
| Interstitial | Post-results (every 3rd match) | Min 3 min gap | NO | DISABLED |
| Banner | Store screen bottom | Always in store | N/A | Shown |
