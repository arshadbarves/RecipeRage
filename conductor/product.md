# Initial Concept

RecipeRage is a fast-paced multiplayer cooking competition where players compete in various game modes to become the ultimate chef! It features a modular, extensible framework with rich social features, including a P2P friends system and real-time multiplayer.

## Vision & Purpose
RecipeRage is designed to be the definitive multiplayer cooking competition experience. It combines high-speed, frantic gameplay with deep social connectivity and long-term progression, all built on a modular and highly extensible technical foundation.

## Target Audience
- **Casual Gamers:** Players looking for accessible, high-energy fun in short bursts.
- **Competitive Players:** Skill-oriented players who want to master mechanics and climb leaderboards.
- **Social Players:** Users who value community, playing with friends, and engaging in social systems like clubs or groups.

## Key Goals
1. **Core Excellence:** Deliver a highly polished, responsive, and addictive core gameplay loop.
2. **Scalable Infrastructure:** Build a robust multiplayer system using Netcode for GameObjects and EOS that ensures a seamless experience.
3. **Engaging Meta-Game:** Create a meaningful progression system that keeps players coming back.

## Priority Features
- **Real-time Multiplayer:** Supporting 2-4 players in chaotic cooking matches.
- **Social Hub:** A robust friends list, online status tracking, and P2P-powered chat.
- **Progression & Customization:** Player leveling, character/cosmetic unlocks, and a modular shop system.
- **EOS Authentication:** Production-ready authentication flow using Epic Online Services Device ID.

## Competitive Advantages
- **Native Social Experience:** Built-in P2P communication and deep EOS integration for low-latency social interactions.
- **Developer-Friendly Extensibility:** A modular architecture (VContainer/UniTask) that makes adding content fast and reliable.
- **Platform Agnostic:** Seamless cross-auth and cross-play capabilities.

## Visual & Aesthetic Direction
A unique blend of **Whimsical Stylization** and **Modern Industrial** design. Expect vibrant, toon-shaded characters and environments paired with high-quality materials and professional kitchen aesthetics to create a polished yet fun atmosphere.
