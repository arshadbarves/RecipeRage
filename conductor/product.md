# Initial Concept
RecipeRage is a fast-paced multiplayer cooking competition where players compete in various game modes to become the ultimate chef!

# Product Guide

## Target Users
RecipeRage is designed to appeal to a broad spectrum of gamers, including:
- **Competitive Gamers:** Players seeking high-stakes, fast-paced multiplayer action and leaderboard dominance.
- **Casual Players:** Those who enjoy the social and cooperative aspects of cooking games in a fun, accessible environment.
- **Mobile Gamers:** Users interested in quick, session-based competitive play that fits their on-the-go lifestyle.

## Player Goals
Players are motivated by several core objectives:
- **Mastery and Competition:** Climbing the competitive leaderboards by excelling in diverse game modes like Classic, Time Attack, and Team Battle.
- **Progression and Customization:** Advancing through the Season Pass to unlock exclusive character skins, emotes, and power-ups.
- **Social Connectivity:** Building a network of friends and collaborating through integrated chat, friend management, and game invites.

## Core Features & Development Priority
The development will follow a phased approach, prioritizing infrastructure and UI before core gameplay:

1.  **Phase 1: Foundation & UI System:**
    -   **Backend Integration:** Full integration of Epic Online Services (EOS) for authentication and friends, and Firebase for remote config.
    -   **UI Architecture:** A robust, modular Tab-based UI system (SOLID compliant) handling login, main menu, friends, and comprehensive settings.
    -   **Social Features:** Chat and party management UI.
    -   **Control Customization:** Interactive HUD editor for mobile layout optimization.

2.  **Phase 2: Core Gameplay:**
    -   **Game Modes:** Implementation of Classic, Time Attack, and Team Battle modes.
    -   **Character System:** Playable characters with unique abilities and interactions.

## Economy and Progression
The game employs a sustainable free-to-play model:
- **In-Game Shop:** A marketplace for cosmetic items and power-ups.
- **Season Pass:** A tiered progression system offering rewards for both free and premium players.
- **Dual-Currency System:** Soft currency earned through gameplay and hard currency available for purchase.
- **Rewarded Ads:** Optional video ads that allow players to earn currency or store items.

## Long-Term Vision
Our long-term commitment is to ensure the game's longevity through:
- **Continuous Content Expansion:** Regularly introducing new game modes, characters, and seasonal updates to keep the community engaged.
